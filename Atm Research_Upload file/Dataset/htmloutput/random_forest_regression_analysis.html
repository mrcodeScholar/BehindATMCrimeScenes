Random Forest Regression Analysis

Algorithm Used: Random Forest Regression

Random Forest Regression is an ensemble learning method for regression that operates by constructing a multitude of decision trees during training and outputting the mean prediction of the individual trees.

Mean Squared Error:

The mean squared error (MSE) measures the average of the squares of the errors, or deviations, between the predicted values and the actual values. In this analysis, the MSE is approximately 0.0745.
